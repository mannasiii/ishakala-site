About Us

Meet the Artist – Gurudas Sadanand Khandeparkar With over 30 years of experience in the world of art, I am deeply committed to preserving and promoting traditional, spiritual, and contemporary artistic expressions. Holding a G.D. Art (Painting) degree, I have dedicated my life to mentoring students, reviving cultural art forms, and exploring the spiritual aspects of creativity.

As the founder of Ishakala Studio, my mission is to inspire and educate art lovers about the profound impact of art on the mind, soul, and energy field. My work extends beyond conventional art, focusing on the spiritual essence of creation, particularly through Sattvik Ganesha idol-making, Mandala art, and divine compositions.Through Ishakala Studio, I strive to create a space where art becomes a medium of transformation and inner harmony. Each creation is infused with devotion and intention, reflecting the deep connection between artistic expression and spiritual energy.

Ishakala Studio

At Ishakala Studio, we offer a harmonious blend of creativity, spirituality, and artistic excellence through our diverse range of services. Our Online Art Classes allow learners of all levels to explore traditional, spiritual, and modern techniques from the comfort of their homes, guided by expert mentorship.
